FAQ

How do I access my purchase?
To access your Go Laser Cut purchases, simply login to your account and click “Downloads”.
Can I re-download my designs?
Absolutely. You may access your purchased designs at any time by logging in to your Go Laser Cut Account and clicking “Downloads”. There is no limit to how many times you can download each item.
Where do designs I purchase from Go Laser Cut go?
All designs purchased from Go Laser Cut – including those in bundles – are stored in your Go Laser Cut Account. Sign into your account > click “My Account” > Orders to view your order history and re-download any purchases.
I didn't receive my bundle. Instead, I received only a PDF file. How can I download all types of files from the bundle?
To download the archive containing all file formats, please open the PDF file and follow the provided link. If you still cannot download the archive using this method, please contact our Help Center.
Before using the product, please unzip the archive.
Do I need to create an account on Go laser Cut?
In order to complete your purchase on Go Laser Cut, we need you to create an account. If you are a new customer, you can create an account during the checkout process.
Can I use this design for commercial use (to sell)?
Absolutely you can. Please be sure to read the Commercial License for a full description of how you can use the items you purchase from Go Laser Cut.
Can I resell a digital copy of your files?
No, we do not allow the resale of any digital files purchased from our website.
What is the refund policy for digital downloads?
Due to the nature of our digital products, we are unable to offer refunds for digital downloads. All sales are final*
Is there a Go Laser Cut app?
While there is currently no official Go Laser Cut app available, you can still access it on any of your devices by visiting our website (ссылка на сайт). We will keep you updated if and when we develop an app in the future.
What format are the items on Go Laser Cut?
We offer several files formats SVG, PNG, PDF, DXF, EPS, CDR, JPG. please refer to each item’s description.
Will your files work with my laser machine?
If your software supports any of my file types, you can use them with your laser cutting machine
Do you have a license or permit?
Of course! You can find information about our license or permit here: ссылка на лицензию
How can I determine the size of the final product? Can I adjust this design to fit my needs?
If you would like to find out the size of the final product, please contact our customer support team. You can also contact the help center for any questions about the final product size.
I’m having issues with the assembly process and file size. Could you please advise me on what I should do to resolve these issues?

If the SVG file opens with incorrect dimensions in Lightburn, you need to select 72 DPI Illustrator in the settings or try to open a PDF


If the DXF does not open correctly in RDWorks, you can resave the SVG in the Illustrator with the following settings AutoCAD 2000/LT2000 version Scale:1 Millimeters=1

If you are working with CorelDRAW use an EPS file, it will maintain the correct dimensions.

You can re-save the SVG file so that it opens with the correct dimensions in all programs using XTool Creative Space.

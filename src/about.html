Our Story

About

Go Laser Cut, based in the heart of Israel, was founded by three creative designers with a passion for design. With a wealth of experience in the industry, we recognized the difficulty in finding high-quality laser cutting designs. We are decided to create Go Laser Cut to make it easier for people to access and use laser cutting files. We are dedicated to providing you with the best laser cutting files available. Let us help you bring your ideas to life with our extensive collection of designs.
“ We have come together to bring the crafters even more amazing designs in one place. “

The T-Store Design

The store with laser digital designs, known as The T-Store Design, was founded in 2022 by passionate innovator with a background in business and design. His vision was to revolutionize traditional art and product customization by harnessing the power of laser technology. What started as a small shop has now grown into a premier destination for cutting- edge laser-cut and laser-engraved products.

Fly Design

Fly Design, a store specializing in laser-digital designs, was founded in 2020 by a passionate woman with a love for art. She aims to offer unique laser-cut and laser-engraved products that people will love. What started as a hobby has now become the top source for creative and stylish laser artwork. Fly Design continues to inspire and empower its customers with its originality, skilled craftsmanship and personalized designs.

Digital Idea

Digital Idea is a store that was founded in 2020 and focuses on laser-cut designs. The store was started by an artist who loves art and wanted to create precise and innovative artwork. Her goal was to bring a new kind of art to life through detailed laser cuts. Starting as an experiment, the store has grown into a place where people can find modern, digitally created designs. Digital Idea continues to inspire and excite fans with its commitment to advanced techniques, futuristic designs, and personalized digital artwork.
We offer free design resources each week and a marketplace which allows graphic designers to register and sell their products.
